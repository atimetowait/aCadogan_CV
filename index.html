Can strict, prosed words, stand on their own?


If one were to create, better in—chasms of thought.

Where should it go?

For faltering of a clause, still, remains simply...astute.
Up to the mercy of the void, or...
The larger internet in its, better half.

Spoken in our menial phrases,
Back, forth, again, & again.
If thrust into the light, to shine—pointless.
For far too many go back, to forth,

Worry not,
Let them


Fester.
