Introduction

  Hey there!!
My name is Alex Cadogan (She/hers) - a creative, musician, dev, 
All with a heart! And all as, myself.

I've lived in Washington D.C. for the start of my life, I finished 7 years of Chemo, ran a successful endevour within the music industry.
From mastering credits, to 2200 songs...to still, signing record deals. I am confident to have seen what is 'that world'.

I had to make a decision, not only at 17...when I embarked on a journey of Chemo therapy but, "Who do I, want to be?" 
-I asked myself it, and with no doubt in my mind. I threw myself into a creative project. I then named, 'atimetowait'.

In a way of signaling the times, to hold myself accountable, and to still...be whom I, wish to be!!
Earnesty, morals, and a creative flare is what I bring to not only Cybersecurity, 

But to life, to culture, and to this idea that, you can still...be whoever you want, maybe only online at times!!
But who you wish to be in person, as well.
